About Car

This car charges using wireless power transfer. Below is a picture showing how the system works and how it is set up on the track. Wireless power transfer works by having the transmitter device, driven by electric power from a power source, generate a time-varying electromagnetic field, which transmits power across space to a receiver device. In this case, the electromagnetic field is generated by inductive coupling between coils of wire.
